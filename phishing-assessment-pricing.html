Phishing Assessment Pricing

PhishU publishes one-time phishing assessment pricing because buyers should be able to understand the budget before scheduling a call. These tiers cover a single org and include reporting and training.

Public starting prices

This page is intentionally consulting-specific. If you are comparing the annual software license against one-time assessment work, see the full PhishU pricing page.

Good fit for a first evaluation or a tightly scoped engagement.

  • One campaign
  • Up to 500 targets
  • Technical report and analytics
  • Training included

More room for department coverage, pretext variation, or a second campaign.

Best fit when the buyer wants broader one-time coverage in the same org.

What is included in every tier

  • Framework-backed delivery instead of a stitched-together one-off process
  • Analytics and evidence suitable for internal stakeholders
  • Training included at no additional cost
  • Single-org engagement scope
  • Additional scoping available when you need more targets, more campaigns, or wider coverage

What usually changes the price

  • Target count
  • Number of campaigns and pretexts
  • Technique depth and realism requirements
  • Reporting expectations and follow-up needs
  • Whether the buyer would be better served by the annual platform instead

When to choose consulting vs the full platform

Choose consulting when the goal is to have PhishU run the work and deliver the result. Choose the annual platform when the team wants to run campaigns repeatedly, support multiple orgs, or productize phishing as an ongoing internal or client-facing program.

PhishU Framework platform pricing is $12,000 per year for the first org and $1,000 for each additional org, with training included. That is often the better fit once the buyer expects recurring use.
