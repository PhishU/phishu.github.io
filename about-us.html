About PhishU

Built by a phishing expert, for red teams. Training is free because we believe in it.

The Origin Story

PhishU started in 2014 as an open-source project born out of necessity. Our Flounder and CEO, Curtis Brazzell, was spending 60-80 hours on every spear-phishing assessment. Most of that time went to infrastructure, reconnaissance, pretext development, and campaign content, not the social engineering itself.

The offensive tools available at the time required stitching together separate projects with tons of setup and tweaking. The commercial platforms were allow-listed, didn't capture credentials or sessions, didn't support custom domains, and weren't built for red teams. The time, cost, and expertise required to run a quality phishing engagement meant many consulting firms stopped offering spear-phishing services altogether.

Meanwhile, phishing remained the number one attack path. Attackers didn't stop. Defenders just lost the ability to test against it realistically.

That open-source project evolved over the years into the PhishU Framework: a single platform that handles the full engagement lifecycle. Domain acquisition, landing pages, email delivery, evasion, credential capture, session hijacking, analytics, branded reports with evidence, and conditional training. What used to take 60-80 hours now takes a few hours of total effort, with better results.

Research-Driven Development

Curtis has spent over a decade doing original security research and publishing novel phishing and offensive security techniques. From phishing password managers and bypassing MFA to AI-generated phishing PoCs and building evasion blocklists that keep landing pages hidden from security bots.

Every technique researched and published has gone directly into the PhishU Framework. Adversary-in-the-Middle (AiTM) session hijacking, Browser-in-Browser (BiB), ClickFix, OAuth Consent Grant with persistent token capture, Device Code Phishing. When a new technique trends in the wild, it gets added to the platform so red teams can test it immediately and so employees get trained on what real attacks actually look like.

AI assists throughout the workflow, from reconnaissance and campaign planning through email generation, deliverability analysis, report writing, and training content. The platform is a living tool that gets better with every engagement and every new threat.

Training Is Free Because We Believe In It

Security awareness training is included in every PhishU license at no additional cost. No per-seat fees. No add-on charges. No usage limits. It is also entirely optional.

This isn't a packaging decision. It's a conviction. Curtis believes that when people understand what caught them, they get better at spotting the next attempt. Charging separately for training creates a perverse incentive: the operator skips training to save money, the targets never learn, and the next campaign has the same click rate. That's a failure for everyone except the vendor collecting the add-on fee.

PhishU's training is different from generic awareness modules. It's conditional, campaign-specific, and personalized. Each recipient sees the actual email and landing page they encountered, with annotations pointing out the red flags they missed. That moment of recognition is what changes behavior. Not a 30-minute video about password hygiene.

The Cybersecurity ABCs

Each page maps a real attack technique to a custom watercolor sea creature illustration. The books were dedicated to his kids and born from the same belief that drives the platform: making security concepts accessible actually matters.
