Why buyers choose KnowBe4

KnowBe4 is a strong fit for organizations that want a large training-content catalog and a mature internal awareness program centered around employee education workflows.

Why buyers choose PhishU Framework

PhishU stands out when the buyer wants realistic, current tradecraft, external-target support, transparent platform pricing, and training generated from what happened in the campaign instead of a generic content assignment.

The safest wedge is often not “replace KnowBe4 immediately.” It is “run PhishU Framework alongside your existing awareness program and measure what happens when the simulation goes through real defenses and uses current tradecraft.”
