Doppelganger Domain Acquisition

PhishU Framework does not treat lookalike domains as an external prerequisite. The platform suggests doppelganger domains similar to the target organization, lets operators acquire them directly in the app, and then use them immediately for landing page URLs, sender identity, and the URL builder.

Track Domain Health and Status

The domain workflow is not just about acquisition. Framework users can also review domain status directly in the app so they can see which domains are usable, which need attention, and how the domain pool is evolving over time.
