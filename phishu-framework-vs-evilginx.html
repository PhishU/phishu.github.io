Choose Evilginx if...

You specifically want a proxy component and you are comfortable handling the rest of the phishing operation yourself.

Choose PhishU Framework if...

You want the proxy plus domain provisioning, landing pages, email delivery, evasion, analytics, training, reporting, and multi-org management in one web-based product.
