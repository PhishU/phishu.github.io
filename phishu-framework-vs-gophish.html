PhishU Framework vs GoPhish

GoPhish is a respected open-source phishing toolkit. PhishU Framework is a hosted platform built to remove the operational glue around realistic phishing engagements. The practical question is simple: do you want a toolkit you assemble around, or a broader workflow that is already there?

Choose GoPhish if...

You want an open-source toolkit, you are comfortable self-hosting, and you do not mind assembling adjacent capabilities like advanced landing pages, reporting, training, and infrastructure management yourself.

Choose PhishU Framework if...

You want one platform that already includes realistic phishing workflows, newer techniques, training, reporting, hosted maintenance, and multi-org operations for client or internal use.

Comparison note: GoPhish is a trademark of its respective owner. This comparison is based on public documentation and normal operator workflows as of March 2026. Product capabilities may change over time.

The biggest difference is not just feature count. It is operational burden. GoPhish gives operators a good starting toolkit. PhishU Framework aims to replace the stitched-together toolchain around realistic phishing engagements with one maintained product.
